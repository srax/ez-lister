# ezlist (ez-lister) — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 28 June 2026_

ezlist is a Chrome extension that helps a user re-post vehicle listings from a car
dealership website to Facebook Marketplace.

## What the extension does

On the dealership website, ezlist reads publicly-displayed vehicle details (year, make,
model, trim, price, mileage, colours, fuel type, and photos) from the page you are
viewing. It stores this vehicle "draft" locally on your device and uses it to pre-fill
Facebook Marketplace's "Vehicle for sale" form in your own logged-in browser session.
You review and publish the listing yourself.

## Data we handle

- The only data ezlist handles is the public vehicle-listing content and photos described
  above, used solely to pre-fill your Facebook Marketplace listing.
- ezlist does **not** collect personal information, passwords, payment details, location,
  browsing history, or analytics.
- ezlist **never** asks for or stores your Facebook username or password. It works within
  your existing, logged-in Facebook session.

## Data sharing and storage

- ezlist sends **no data to the developer or to any third-party server**. Vehicle data
  stays on your device; it enters Facebook Marketplace only by your own action when you
  create a listing.
- The vehicle draft is stored locally (via Chrome's storage) and is overwritten each time
  you list a new vehicle.

## Our commitments

- We do not sell or transfer your data to third parties.
- We do not use or transfer your data for any purpose unrelated to the extension's single
  purpose.
- We do not use your data to determine creditworthiness or for lending purposes.
